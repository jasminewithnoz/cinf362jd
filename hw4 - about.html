About

A passion for gaming turned into an app!
I started playing Magic: The Gathering with my friends a couple of months ago and had a bunch of fun getting into the franchise. However, there was a big learning curve. There were several mechanics, rules, and playstyles to learn and it was honestly really intimidating. So, when I was given the assignment to make an application prototype for the class CINF302, I took that challenge and created THE WIZARD'S HANDBOOK!
The Development Process!
Because THE WIZARD'S HANDBOOK is still prototype and because my knowledge on coding applications are very small, I decided to use Figma when it comes to designing as it's free for students and makes prototyping simple!
